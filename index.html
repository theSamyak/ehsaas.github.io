Who We Are

In a world that often focuses on barriers, Ehsaas focuses on bridges. We aims to bridge the gap between different abilities and create a harmonious connection to nature, surroundings, and emotions

At EHSAAS, we believe that everyone deserves to experience the beauty and wonder of the world. Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to create an app that allows blind individuals to 'see' through words. EHSAAS is the embodiment of that idea, a companion that transforms visual moments into poetic experiences, nurturing independence, happiness, and a renewed sense of purpose.

Our Values

What we believe in

World Inclusiveness

With our belief in "Vasudev Kutumbakam" we are working towards breaking down barriers and creating a global community where individuals of all abilities can communicate, explore, and connect. Our app strives to foster a sense of unity that transcends borders, languages, and differences.

Empathy drives us to extend a helping hand to those in need. Ehsaas is dedicated to enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities, enabling them to experience the world's beauty and opportunities.

Ehsaas is not just about personal growth; it's about contributing to the greater good. We're committed to the welfare of our country and society.
