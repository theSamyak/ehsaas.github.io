Who We Are

In a world that often focuses on barriers, Ehsaas focuses on bridges. We aims to bridge the gap between different abilities and create a harmonious connection to nature, surroundings, and emotions

At EHSAAS, we believe that everyone deserves to experience the beauty and wonder of the world. Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to create an app that allows blind individuals to 'see' through words. EHSAAS is the embodiment of that idea, a companion that transforms visual moments into poetic experiences, nurturing independence, happiness, and a renewed sense of purpose.
